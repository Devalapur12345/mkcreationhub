## Step-by-Step Guide to Get on Google Search

### STEP 1: Deploy Your Website (5 minutes)
**What to do:**
1. Click "Publish" button in v0
2. Connect your custom domain (optional but recommended)
3. Wait for deployment to complete

**Verify:**
- Your website should be live and accessible
- Test by visiting your domain in a browser

---

### STEP 2: Google Search Console Setup (10 minutes)
**What to do:**
1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Click "Add property"
3. Enter your website domain
4. Verify ownership by:
   - Adding HTML meta tag to your site, OR
   - Adding DNS record to your domain
5. Click "Verify"

**What happens next:**
- Google starts crawling your website
- You can see search queries that bring people to your site
- Monitor performance and errors

---

### STEP 3: Submit Your Sitemap (2 minutes)
**What to do:**
1. Go to Google Search Console
2. Click "Sitemaps" in the left menu
3. Click "Add/test sitemap"
4. Enter: `yourwebsite.com/sitemap.xml`
5. Click "Submit"

**What it does:**
- Tells Google about all your pages
- Helps Google index your pages faster
- Ensures all pages are discoverable

---

### STEP 4: Setup Google My Business (15 minutes)
**IMPORTANT: This is crucial for local search!**

**What to do:**
1. Go to: https://www.google.com/business
2. Click "Create account"
3. Enter business name: "MK Creations Hub"
4. Add your business category: "Gift Shop" or "Event Planning Service"
5. Enter your business address:
   - Azam Nager, Belagavi, Karnataka
6. Enter your phone: +91 7795440217
7. Add business hours:
   - Monday-Saturday: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
   - Sunday: Closed
8. Add business description
9. Upload photos of your work (use your gallery images)
10. Wait for verification (Google will call or send postcard)

**Why this matters:**
- Shows up in Google Maps
- Shows up when people search locally
- Displays your business info prominently
- People can see your photos and reviews

---

### STEP 5: Monitor Your Rankings (Weekly)
**What to do:**
1. Open Google Search Console weekly
2. Check "Performance" section
3. Note which keywords bring visitors
4. See how many times you appear in search results

**Key metrics to track:**
- Clicks to your site
- Average position in search results
- Search impressions (how many times shown)
- Click-through rate (CTR)

## Troubleshooting: Not Showing Up?

### Issue 1: Website Not in Google Index
**Solution:**
1. Go to Google Search Console
2. Request indexing for your homepage
3. Submit sitemap again
4. Wait 1-2 weeks

### Issue 2: Low Rankings (Beyond Page 1)
**Solution:**
- Needs 4-6 weeks of optimization
- Add more content to your website
- Get more customer reviews
- Build Google My Business profile

### Issue 3: Appearing but No Clicks
**Solution:**
- Improve your meta description (makes search results more appealing)
- Make title more engaging
- Add special keywords to description
