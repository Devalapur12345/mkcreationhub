## How to Get Your Website in Google Search Results

### Option 1: Quick Setup (Recommended for Beginners)

1. **Deploy to Vercel** (1 minute)
   - Click "Publish" button in v0
   - Or use: `vercel deploy`

2. **Verify in Google** (5 minutes)
   - Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
   - Add your domain
   - Verify ownership

3. **Submit Sitemap** (1 minute)
   - In Google Search Console
   - Add: `yourdomain.com/sitemap.xml`

4. **Set Up Google My Business** (15 minutes)
   - Go to https://www.google.com/business
   - Add your business info
   - Add phone, address, hours
   - Add photos of your gift packages

**Wait 1-2 weeks** → Your website appears in Google!
